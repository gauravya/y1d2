Tom really loves his balcony. He used to sit, ponder, drink a beverage and enjoy the sun. Of course, it is Glasgow. The sun is hardly to be seen here. As such this was a rare moment of catching Tom taking in the moment.

Out from the balcony you can see the West End. The bougie part of Glasgow. It is where the pretty people are. It is where I spend most of my time. If you ever visit Glasgow you must visit the West End.
